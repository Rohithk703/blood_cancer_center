Available Services

Comprehensive hematology and blood cancer services under one roof.

🩸

Blood Cancer Diagnosis

Evaluation and staging for leukemia, lymphoma, myeloma, and related conditions.

🔬

Hematology Consultation

Expert opinion on anemia, clotting disorders, and other blood-related illnesses.

Chemotherapy & Day Care

Targeted & Supportive Therapy

Patient & Family Counseling
